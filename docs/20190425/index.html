Quarter Final Analysis and Predictions – Champions League
Victoria Chen '21, Sander Kuijs '21, and Michael Zhang '21
After a month-long wait, the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) Champions League finally returned with the quarterfinal round. The first legs took place on April 9th and 10th, providing just six goals. No game had more than two goals scored, foreshadowing how tight this year’s contest will be. Of the remaining eight teams, four are from England, while Spain, Portugal, Italy, and the Netherlands each present one team. As it stands, Liverpool, Juventus, Tottenham Hotspur, and Barcelona will be headed to the semi-finals.
