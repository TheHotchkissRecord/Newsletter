Welcome to the last issue of the CXXII Editorial Board!
Saturday, March 27, 2021
-
Lakeville, CT
Welcome to the last issue of the CXXII Editorial Board! In this issue, we cover community members’ response to the Atlanta shooting, the start of Revisit Day programming, sustainability on campus, and more. In an editorial, the Editors-in-Chief reflect on their past year with The Record. We hope you have enjoyed and learned from the journalism over the past year, and encourage you to contribute and look forward to the upcoming issues under the new CXXIII Editorial Board. Have a great weekend.

- The Record Executive Board
